TheRain Launches in Yaoundé — Cameroon's First Smart Ride Platform

June 1, 2026
Yaoundé, Cameroon
5 min read
We are thrilled to announce the official launch of TheRain in Yaoundé, Cameroon's capital city. This marks a historic milestone in our mission to transform transportation across Cameroon and Africa.

A New Era of Smart Transportation

TheRain is not just another ride-hailing app. It is a complete intelligent transportation ecosystem designed specifically for Cameroon and scalable across Africa. From the bustling streets of Yaoundé to the commercial hub of Douala, TheRain is bringing world-class transportation technology to Cameroonian cities.

What TheRain Offers

  • Classique Ride — Affordable everyday rides across the city
  • VIP Ride — Premium vehicles with top-rated drivers
  • Delivery — Fast and secure package delivery
  • School Transport — Safe, monitored transport for children
  • Fleet Management — Enterprise solutions for businesses
From Our CEO
"TheRain was born from a simple vision — to make transportation in Cameroon safe, smart, and accessible to everyone. Today, as we launch in Yaoundé, we are one step closer to that vision. This is just the beginning." — Ndzenkongang Majid Che, CEO & Founder

Safety at the Core

Every TheRain vehicle is equipped with CCTV cameras, GPS tracking, and an emergency SOS system. All drivers undergo rigorous AI-powered identity verification, face matching, and background checks before being approved on the platform.

Payment Options for Every Cameroonian

TheRain supports all major payment methods used in Cameroon including MTN Mobile Money, Orange Money, Visa cards, bank transfers, and cash — making it accessible to everyone regardless of their preferred payment method.

What's Next?

Following our successful launch in Yaoundé and Douala, TheRain plans to expand to Bamenda, Buea, Limbe, and eventually all 10 regions of Cameroon. Our vision is to become Africa's leading intelligent transportation ecosystem.
