Privacy Policy — Rider
TheRain is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our rider services in Cameroon.
Information We Collect
  • Full name, phone number, and email address during registration
  • Real-time GPS location during active rides and bookings
  • Payment information (MTN MOMO, Orange Money, Visa card details)
  • Ride history, ratings, and feedback submitted
  • Device information, IP address, and app usage data
  • Emergency contact information provided by the rider
How We Use Your Information
  • To match you with the nearest available verified driver
  • To process payments and send receipts via SMS or email
  • To provide real-time ride tracking and safety features
  • To improve our services through analytics and feedback
  • To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and safety violations
Guest Rider Privacy
Guest riders who book without an account are assigned a temporary GUEST_ID. Their ride data is stored temporarily and can be recovered if they create an account later. Guest data is automatically deleted after 90 days of inactivity.
Data Sharing
TheRain does not sell your personal data to third parties. We may share limited data with:
  • Assigned drivers (name, pickup location, and contact number only)
  • Payment processors for transaction verification
  • Law enforcement authorities when legally required
  • Emergency services during SOS activations

CCTV Policy — Rider
All TheRain vehicles are equipped with interior and exterior CCTV cameras for the safety of both riders and drivers. By using TheRain services, you consent to being recorded during your ride.
What is Recorded
  • Interior video footage of the vehicle cabin during rides

Storage & Access
