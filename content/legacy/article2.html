How TheRain School Transport Keeps Your Children Safe Every Day

May 28, 2026
Cameroon
6 min read
Every parent wants to know their child got to school safely. TheRain School Transport gives you exactly that — a verified driver, a confirmed pickup, and live tracking on your phone from departure to drop-off.

Our Multi-Layer Safety System

CCTV Monitoring
Every school transport vehicle has interior and exterior cameras recording throughout the journey.
Live Parent Tracking
Parents can track their child's vehicle in real-time directly from the TheRain app.
Pickup PIN Verification
A unique daily PIN must be verified before any child is released to the driver.
QR Code Confirmation
QR codes provide an additional layer of guardian verification at pickup and dropoff.
Arrival Notifications
Parents receive instant notifications when their child is picked up and dropped off.
Verified School Drivers
All school drivers undergo thorough background checks, AI verification, and special training.

How the Pickup PIN Works

Every morning, parents receive a unique 4-digit PIN through the TheRain app. This PIN is valid only for that day and must be shared with the driver before the child boards the vehicle. The driver scans the QR code or enters the PIN to confirm the pickup — ensuring no child is ever handed to the wrong person.

Parent Peace of Mind
With TheRain School Transport, you can watch your child's journey from home to school and back — in real-time, on your phone. You'll know exactly when they board, where they are, and when they arrive safely.
